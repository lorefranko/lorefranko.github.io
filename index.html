Because design stuff is fun!

This is Me!

I like being in front of graphic trends, get soak in what others are doing, know what others are doing for the world at present, and try to learn from all assessing what might work and what does not. I do not like to get stuck, I consider myself critical of my work, and quite demanding, hence the need to retrain even slightly with each new project I do.
