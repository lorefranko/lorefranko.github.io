Hi · Hello · Bonjour · Hola

Hi, I'm Alyson. I am a designer, illustrator and creative living in Ottawa, Canada. I am passionate about my creativity and spreading it around. I like to use my creativity to help others feel confident about what they represent or support. A continuous learning environment of design keeps me on my toes. I'm driven by the challenges I face. When I'm not designing I love to play with my kids and the family dog, an awkward German Sheppard, or snuggle up and watch a movie at home. Whenever possible, I like to check out local Food Trucks and other delicious food establishments.
